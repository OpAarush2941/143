Ping Pong Game (Table Tennis)



Game Developed By Aarush Bhagwat

Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Move Yourself aproximately 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist up or down to reflect the ping pong ball. A red dot should apear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press the play button and move 3 to 4 feet away.
  5. The red paddel is your paddel. It will move by your wrist movement.
